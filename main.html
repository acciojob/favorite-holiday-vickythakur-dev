Bali, Indonesia


Bali is a beautiful island in Indonesia famous for its stunning beaches, lush rice terraces, ancient temples, and rich culture. It is one of the most popular holiday destinations in the world, offering something for every type of traveller.

Top 5 Attractions

  1. Ubud Monkey Forest — a magical nature reserve full of wild monkeys and ancient temples.
  2. Tanah Lot Temple — a famous sea temple built on a rock surrounded by ocean waves.
  3. Tegallalang Rice Terraces — breathtaking green terraces carved into the hillside near Ubud.
  4. Mount Batur — an active volcano offering stunning sunrise views from the top.
  5. Seminyak Beach — a beautiful sandy beach perfect for surfing, sunsets, and relaxation.

Must-Try Foods

  • Nasi Goreng — Indonesia's famous fried rice dish served with egg and crackers.
  • Babi Guling — a traditional roasted suckling pig packed with Balinese spices.
  • Satay — grilled meat skewers served with a rich and creamy peanut sauce.
  • Lawar — a traditional Balinese dish made with minced meat, coconut, and vegetables.
  • Pisang Goreng — crispy fried bananas, a popular and delicious local snack.
