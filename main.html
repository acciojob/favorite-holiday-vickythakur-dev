Bali, Indonesia


Bali is a breathtaking island paradise nestled in the heart of Indonesia, known for its lush green rice terraces, ancient Hindu temples, vibrant arts scene, and warm tropical beaches. Whether you seek spiritual retreat, adventure, or pure relaxation, Bali offers an unforgettable experience for every kind of traveller.

Top 5 Attractions

  1. Ubud Monkey Forest — a sacred sanctuary home to hundreds of playful long-tailed macaques surrounded by ancient temple ruins.
  2. Tanah Lot Temple — an iconic sea temple perched dramatically on a rocky outcrop, especially stunning at sunset.
  3. Tegallalang Rice Terraces — stunning cascading green paddies carved into the hillside near Ubud.
  4. Mount Batur — a magnificent active volcano perfect for an early-morning trek with panoramic sunrise views.
  5. Seminyak Beach — Bali's most fashionable stretch of golden sand, lined with beach clubs and fine dining.

Must-Try Foods

  • Nasi Goreng — Bali's beloved fried rice, served with a fried egg, crackers, and spicy sambal.
  • Babi Guling — a slow-roasted suckling pig stuffed with traditional spices, a true Balinese delicacy.
  • Satay (Sate) — grilled skewers of seasoned meat served with rich peanut sauce.
  • Lawar — a traditional Balinese mix of minced meat, vegetables, coconut, and aromatic spices.
  • Pisang Goreng — crispy fried bananas, a simple yet irresistible snack enjoyed any time of day.
